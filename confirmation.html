Who This Is and Is Not For

This call is for you if:

  • You run a B2B business doing $50K/month or more in revenue
  • You're a founder, agency owner, consultant, recruiter, or service provider
  • Your buyers are on LinkedIn and you sell a service north of $2K
  • Your online presence doesn't match your actual ability - you're great at what you do but nobody knows it
  • You want a predictable pipeline that compounds, not referrals

This call is not for you if:

  • You're pre-revenue or under $50K/month
  • You want a DIY course or templates - we're done-for-you only
  • You need 10 calls by next Friday - the system compounds over 60-90 days
  • You want automated outreach or AI-generated content - we're the opposite of that
  • You're already paying two agencies for LinkedIn and aren't willing to consolidate

If any of the "not for you" criteria apply, please reschedule - this call won't be the right fit, and we'd rather save both of our time. Reschedule here.
